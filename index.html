Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

How does home sample collection work?
Book a slot via our form or WhatsApp. Our certified phlebotomist visits your home with sterile equipment, collects the sample, and delivers it to our partner lab. Reports are shared online within 24–48 hours.
How long do reports take?
Most routine reports are delivered within 24–48 hours. Some specialized tests may take 3–5 days. We will inform you in advance about the turnaround time for your specific test.
Are your phlebotomists certified?
Yes, all our phlebotomists are trained, certified and background-verified professionals. They follow strict safety and hygiene protocols including use of sterile, single-use equipment.
Which areas do you serve?
We currently serve Mumbai, Thane, and Mumbra. We are actively expanding. If your area is not covered, please contact us and we will try our best to accommodate you.
Are your lab partners NABL accredited?
Yes. We are tied up with Suburban Diagnostics, Metropolis Healthcare, and Thyrocare — all NABL and NABH accredited laboratories with a proven track record of accuracy.
How do I receive my reports?
Reports are delivered digitally via WhatsApp and email as a secure PDF link which you can share with your doctor directly. Physical copies are available on request.
Do I need to be fasting for my test?
Some tests like fasting blood glucose, lipid profile, and liver function tests require 8–12 hours of fasting. We will inform you of fasting requirements when you book.
What is your cancellation policy?
You can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 2 hours before the scheduled time at no charge. Contact us via WhatsApp or call for any changes.
